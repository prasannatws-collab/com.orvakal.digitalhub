# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: June 20, 2026**

Welcome to **Orvakal Digital Hub** (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe, secure, and helpful experience using our mobile application. 

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Orvakal Digital Hub mobile application (available for Android and iOS).

---

## 1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

Orvakal Digital Hub is designed to serve the residents, farmers, and visitors of the Orvakal Node. To provide native mobile features, we request access to certain device features.

### A. Location Services (GPS)
* **Permissions Requested:** `ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION` (Precise Location) and `ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION` (Approximate Location).
* **Purpose:** The App features proximity-based sorting. It uses your device’s current coordinates to dynamically calculate the distance to local public resources (such as banks, ATMs, schools, hospitals, post offices, and police stations) and sort them from nearest to farthest.
* **Processing:** **All location-based distance calculations are performed locally on your device.** Your precise coordinates are never transmitted to our servers or stored permanently.

### B. Text-to-Speech Accessibility (Audio)
* **Permissions Requested:** **None.** The App does not request or require microphone or audio recording permissions.
* **Purpose:** To assist rural users and improve accessibility, the App provides a Text-to-Speech audio reader (using native Expo speech engines) to read crop advisories, government schemes, and notifications aloud.
* **Processing:** All speech synthesis is processed locally on the device using standard accessibility engines. No audio is recorded, captured, or transmitted.

### C. Internet and Network State
* **Permissions Requested:** `INTERNET` and `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE`.
* **Purpose:** To fetch real-time updates including:
  * Live weather data from Open-Meteo services.
  * Real-time agriculture mandi prices and minimum support prices (MSP).
  * Recent village notices and job vacancies.
* **Processing:** The App checks your network connection and sends standard API requests to fetch this public utility data. No personal identifiers are attached to these requests.

---

## 2. No User Account or Personal Data Collection

Orvakal Digital Hub is a community utility portal. 
* **No Registration Required:** You do not need to create an account, register, or provide personal details (such as your name, email address, or phone number) to search directories, view mandi rates, check weather, or use utility calculators.
* **User-Generated Submissions:** If you choose to use optional interactive features (such as posting a job, registering as a helper/labourer, or submitting a local grievance to the Panchayat), the details you voluntarily input (e.g., name, phone number, and description) will be transmitted to our secure database solely to process your request.

---

## 3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Disclosure

We respect your privacy.
* **No Commercial Sharing:** We do not sell, rent, trade, or share any user data with commercial third parties or advertisers.
* **Service Providers:** Standard weather requests are queried from Open-Meteo. These requests only contain latitude/longitude coordinates and do not include any device identifiers or personal details.
* **Legal Compliance:** We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

---

## 4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the information submitted through the App (such as grievance reports or job postings). However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

---

## 5. Control Over Permissions

You can manage or revoke the App's permissions at any time through your device's operating system settings:
* **Location:** Turn off Location Services for "Orvakal Digital Hub" if you do not want nearest-to-farthest sorting. The lists will default to alphabetical sorting.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this document.
