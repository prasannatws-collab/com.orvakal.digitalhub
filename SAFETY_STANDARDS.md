# Safety & Child Protection Standards

**Effective Date: June 23, 2026**

Welcome to **Orvakal Digital Hub** (the "App"). We are committed to maintaining a safe, secure, and trusted environment for the residents, farmers, and visitors of the Orvakal Node. We enforce strict policies to protect our community, especially minors, from any form of exploitation, harm, or abuse.

This Safety & Child Protection Standards document outlines our zero-tolerance stance on Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) and details the systems we implement to prevent, detect, and report such content and behavior.

---

## 1. Zero-Tolerance Policy on CSAE
Orvakal Digital Hub maintains an absolute, zero-tolerance policy towards child sexual abuse, exploitation, and any form of abuse targeting minors. 
* **Prohibited Content & Activities:** The creation, hosting, uploading, distribution, sharing, or facilitation of any content related to Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) or Child Sexual Exploitation and Abuse (CSAE) is strictly prohibited.
* **Immediate Actions:** Any account or submission associated with CSAE will be permanently suspended, all related content will be deleted immediately, and the offender’s details will be reported to law enforcement.

---

## 2. Prevention and Detection Mechanisms
To ensure our platform remains safe, we employ proactive measures and detection strategies:
* **Pre-moderation of Community Content:** All submissions to public desks (such as job postings, labour registrations, and community notices) are reviewed and moderated by administrators before they are published to the public feed.
* **Automated Filtering:** We utilize text and keyword filtering systems on text input fields to block inappropriate content, offensive language, and suspicious links.
* **Proactive Monitoring:** App administrators regularly audit interactive boards and community submissions to ensure compliance with our safety standards.

---

## 3. Reporting Mechanisms for Users
We encourage our community to help us keep Orvakal Digital Hub safe. If you encounter any content, job posting, or user behavior that you suspect violates our safety standards, please report it immediately:

* **In-App Reporting:** You can report issues or suspicious posts directly via the **Panchayat Grievance Desk** in the App. Please select "Others" or specify "Safety Violation" in your report.
* **Direct Email Helpline:** Send an email with details (and screenshots if possible) to our dedicated safety contact:

* **Mailing Address:**

All reports are treated with the highest priority and confidentiality.

---

## 4. Compliance and Legal Obligations
We comply fully with local, national, and international laws, regulations, and industry standards:
* **The POCSO Act, 2012:** We strictly adhere to the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act of India.
* **Information Technology Act, 2000:** We comply with applicable intermediary guidelines and IT laws regarding child safety online.
* **Reporting to Authorities:** Under Indian law, we are legally obligated to report any detection of CSAM/CSAE immediately to the police and appropriate authorities, including the **National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal** (https://cybercrime.gov.in) and law enforcement agencies. We fully cooperate with law enforcement investigation requests.

---

## 5. Updates to Our Safety Standards
We may update these Safety & Child Protection Standards from time to time to align with new laws, technologies, or industry best practices. We will notify users of any updates by posting the revised document on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
